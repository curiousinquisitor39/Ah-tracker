Welcome to AH Tracker

Track your LAS Annualised Hours compliance — shifts, nights, weekends, and bank holidays — all in one place.

How to get your GRS calendar link:

1. Log into GRS Mobile
2. Tap the ☰ menu (3 lines, top left)
3. Tap GRS Calendar
4. Tap Request Calendar — this sends an email to your NHS address
5. Open the email and copy the calendar link from the Android section
6. Paste it below ↓

⚠️ Treat this link like a password — it gives access to your shift data. It is stored only on this device and never sent to any server.

Find these figures in your Weekly Update Report:

• Arrangement Start Date — first date in the PERIOD column
• Contract Hours — the CONTRACT HRS column (e.g. 1955.25)
• Total Hours to Work — the TOTAL HRS TO WORK THIS PERIOD column (e.g. 1600.53)

The date you first went onto Annualised Hours — from your original AH arrangement letter. Used to calculate which year you're in and your bank holiday requirements.

First date from the PERIOD column in your Weekly Update Report (e.g. 06/07/2026). Used to calculate your hours target and pro-rata compliance.

From the CONTRACT HRS column — used to calculate your weekly hours and WTE

From the TOTAL HRS TO WORK THIS PERIOD column — this is your compliance target

📲 Install as an App

iPhone (Safari only):
Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom of the screen → tap "Add to Home Screen". The app will appear on your home screen like a native app. Must be opened in Safari, not Chrome.
Android (Chrome):
Tap the three dots menu (⋮) top right → tap "Add to Home Screen". Or tap Install if a banner appears automatically at the bottom of the screen.

All data is stored only on this device. Your iCal link is never shared.
